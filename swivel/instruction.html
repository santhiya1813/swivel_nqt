Examination Rules & Instructions

Please read the following instructions carefully. These rules are strictly enforced in accordance with MNC examination standards.

  • Students must maintain a fixed posture facing the screen at all times.
  • Head movement (left or right) is restricted.
  • Opening new browser tabs or windows is strictly prohibited.
  • Use of external devices, headphones, or secondary screens is not allowed.
  • Copy, paste, print screen, or screenshot actions are disabled.
  • Switching applications or minimizing the browser is not permitted.
  • Ensure a stable internet connection throughout the examination.
  • The examination must be taken in a quiet, well-lit environment.
Violation Handling:
  • Any violation will result in immediate termination of the examination.
  • No re-attempt will be allowed after termination.
